Jollof Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • Gizzards
  • Plantains
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Wash and clean the Gizzards thoroughly
  2. Place the Gizzards inside the cooking Pot add the diced onions,1 seasoning cube Curry powder, Thyme, and Salt to taste. Add water to the same level as the Gizzards and cook for about 40 minutes or until tender.
  3. Preheat some cooking Oil and fry the Gizzards till brown. Remove from Oil and set aside.
  4. Peel and cut the Plantain into cube-like pieces, sprinkle with a pinch of salt and fry till golden brown.
  5. Put about 4 Tablespoons of cooking Oil into a Pan, add and stir-fry the Onions for about 2 minutes. Add the diced Tomatoes and Minced Habanero pepper. Fry for 3 to 5 minutes or anywhere in between.
  6. If too thick add a bit of chicken stock or water to loosen it up if necessary. Add 1 Seasoning Cube, curry powder, Thyme, and Salt to taste.
  7. Finally, add the Gizzards, the Fried Plantains, and the diced bell peppers. Stir together thoroughly, leave to simmer for 3 to 5 minutes and you are done.
  8. Serve and enjoy ”as is” or serve as a side to Rice, Couscous, Spaghetti or any other meal of your choice.
